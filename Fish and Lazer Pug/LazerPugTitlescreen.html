~ LASER PUG - MASTER DINO HUNTER ~







INSTRUCTIONS
 Your name is Cuddles, and you're a proffessional
triceratops hunting pug.
Your job is to hunt as many triceratops as possible.
Use WASD to move, and Q to fire your lasers.
Use caution when near Trikes, they are quite capable of
defending themselves with their horns.
Also, there are rumours of a Carnotaurus on the loose!
Your lasers wont be able to harm the beast, so avoid him
at all costs!
Good Luck!
